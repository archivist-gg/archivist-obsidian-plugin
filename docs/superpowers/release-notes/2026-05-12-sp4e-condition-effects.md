## SP4e — Condition Mechanical Effects

- Conditions now produce visible effects on the sheet:
  - Speed reductions for Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Restrained, Unconscious
  - Disadvantage tags on attacks, saves, ability checks based on active conditions
  - Auto-fail markers on STR/DEX saves for Paralyzed, Petrified, Stunned, Unconscious
  - Action and reaction grayout when Incapacitated
- Exhaustion mechanically applies for both editions:
  - 2014: cumulative effects per level (1: ability check disadvantage; 2: speed halved; 3: attack/save disadvantage; 4: HP max halved; 5: speed 0; 6: death)
  - 2024: linear formula (−2 per level on all d20 tests; −5 ft speed per level; death at 6)
- Hover tooltip on each condition chip lists the mechanical effects.
- Manual overrides on speed / HP max still win over condition effects.
