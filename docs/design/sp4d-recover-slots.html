Try it: untick an expended slot (the ✗ clears and it rings crimson = chosen); the budget counts down by that level and unaffordable slots dim. Untick the lone 3rd to use the whole budget at once, or two 1st‑levels, etc. Press Recover to restore them and spend the use; an empty selection keeps Recover disabled.
