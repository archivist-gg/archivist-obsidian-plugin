# Credits

## Condition icons

The PC character sheet's conditions popover uses icon SVGs.

Current icons (as of SP4) are simple geometric placeholders, marked with
`TODO(SP4 icons)` in `src/modules/pc/assets/condition-icons.ts`. These are
to be replaced with artwork from [game-icons.net](https://game-icons.net/)
(licensed under [CC-BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/)).

When the real art lands, individual attributions will be recorded as JSDoc
on each export in `src/modules/pc/assets/condition-icons.ts`, and the
game-icons.net attribution will be required here and in any shipped
documentation per the CC-BY 3.0 terms.
